# Mini Project Facts

Project title: The Last Lantern

Genre: cinematic fantasy / suspense / messenger chase

Length: 30 seconds

Visual style:

- live-action cinematic realism
- rainy night alley
- low saturation
- warm lantern light
- restrained fantasy effect

Main character:

- Mira
- young adult courier
- practical dark travel coat
- wet hair tucked under hood
- carries sealed brass message tube
- anxious but controlled

Antagonist:

- masked guard
- black lacquer mask
- dark rain cloak
- slow, controlled movement
- blocks the alley exit

Key prop:

- sealed brass message tube
- small enough to hide under coat
- old metal, engraved but not readable
- emits low hum and thin gold light only near the end

Required scene:

- narrow rain-soaked alley
- wet stone ground
- old market walls
- last burning lantern
- background exits visible

Sound style:

- rain
- footsteps
- distant city ambience
- lantern flame flicker
- brass tube hum
- no background music in raw generated clips
