Mode: Seedance-style image-to-video prompt

## Reference responsibilities

Mira character reference controls Mira's face, hood, dark courier coat, body proportion, and anxious but controlled identity. It does not control the alley layout.

Rainy alley scene master controls the narrow wet alley, old market walls, last lantern position, wet stone ground, and night atmosphere. It does not change Mira's costume.

Brass message tube prop reference controls the tube size, old brass material, unreadable engraving, and hidden message function. It does not create a large magical weapon.

## Sound

Rain, distant market creak, wet footsteps stopping, lantern flame flicker, low brass hum, Mira's quiet breath, whispered dialogue. No background music, no BGM, no score, no drums.

## Color and light

Low-saturation rainy night. Warm lantern light is the main source. One-second blue flame flicker is small and controlled. Brass tube glow is thin warm gold only near Mira's hand and face.

## Ending

Hold on Mira's controlled fear after the whispered line for a short beat. Prepare for the next shot where the masked guard appears.

## Forbidden

No large magic explosion. No full blue lighting takeover. No modern objects. No readable text on the tube. No extra main character. No background music. No fast zoom. No anime style. No superhero pose.
