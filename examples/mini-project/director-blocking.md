Segment: 0:08-0:16

Purpose: Mira discovers the message tube is not what she thought.

Dialogue:

Mira whispers: "This was never meant for the king."

Time feasibility:

Pass. One stop action, one prop reaction, one short whispered line.

## Blocking

0:08-0:10

Mira runs into the last lantern pool and stops sharply. Camera is medium close, slightly low, wet wall behind her. Her breath is visible in the rain.

0:10-0:12

The lantern flame flickers blue for one second. Mira looks up first, then down toward the hidden message tube under her coat.

0:12-0:14

Her hand tightens around the brass tube. The tube gives a low hum. No large magic burst. Only a narrow warm reflection appears under her fingers.

0:14-0:16

Mira leans closer and whispers, "This was never meant for the king." End on her controlled fear, not panic.

## Camera

Stable handheld feel. Medium close to close-up. Slight push-in during the whisper. No fast zoom.

## Sound

Rain, distant market signs creaking, lantern flame flicker, low brass hum, Mira's quiet breath, whispered line. No background music.

## Transition

Sound bridge from running footsteps into sudden stop. Cut out after the whispered line, before the guard appears in the next segment.
