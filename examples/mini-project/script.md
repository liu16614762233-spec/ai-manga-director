# Mini Project Script

Title: The Last Lantern

Format: 30-second cinematic AI manga short

Style: live-action cinematic fantasy, low-saturation night market, rain, warm lantern light

## Script

0:00-0:08

A narrow rain-soaked alley. Most lanterns have gone out. A young courier named Mira runs into frame, holding a sealed brass message tube under her coat.

0:08-0:16

She stops below the last burning lantern. The flame flickers blue for one second. Mira realizes the message tube is humming.

Mira whispers:

"This was never meant for the king."

0:16-0:24

Footsteps echo behind her. A masked guard appears at the far end of the alley, not running, just blocking the exit. Rainwater runs down his black lacquer mask.

0:24-0:30

Mira steps backward into the lantern light. The brass tube opens by itself, casting a thin line of gold across her face. Cut to black before the guard reaches her.
